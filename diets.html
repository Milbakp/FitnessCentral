What makes a healty diet?

A healthy diet is one where you consume the appropriate amount of calories while also making sure you have enough of every nutrient. There are macronutrients like carbs,protein and fats( see list to the left) and micronutrients like vitamins and minerals. To learn more about healthy diets visit WHO : healthy diet.
