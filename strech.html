How is streching beneficial?

The point of doing stretching exercises is to improve the flexibility and range of motion of your joints. Having a more flexible body helps to prevent injury during other exercises and can help you maintain a better posture.


Preventing injury

Although stretching can prevent injury. You have to make sure not to get injured while stretching. Some safety measures to take include:

  • Warming up 5-10 minutes beforehand.
  • Keep your stretches low and gentle.
  • If you start to feel pain stop stretching.
