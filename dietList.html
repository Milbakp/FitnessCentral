Want to try some exotic diets?

Mediterranean diet

This diet focuses on minimising processed foods while increasing intake of more traditional foods eaten in Greece and Italy.

DASH diet

A diet that helps to prevent high blood pressure. It consists of eating fruits, vegetables,whole grain and lean meat.

WW diet

By giving points to specific food groups. The WW diet makes you eat within your daily point allowance.

Intermittent fasting

This dietary strategy involves periods of fasting and eating in a cycle. Meaning periods of no eating and eating.
